# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2026

GOAT QR Generator respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our web application.

## 1. Information We Collect

GOAT QR Generator does **not** collect, store, or process any personal data.

- No user accounts
- No login required
- No tracking cookies
- No analytics that identify users

QR codes are generated instantly and are **not stored** on our servers.

## 2. How the Application Works

When you enter a URL to generate a QR code:
- The data is processed temporarily to generate the QR code
- The data is not saved, logged, or shared
- All processing is done securely and automatically

## 3. Cookies

GOAT QR Generator does **not** use cookies.

## 4. Third-Party Services

This application is hosted on **Vercel**. Vercel may collect limited technical information (such as IP address or request metadata) as part of standard hosting and security operations.

Please refer to Vercel’s Privacy Policy for more information.

GOAT QR Generator itself does not integrate any third-party analytics, ads, or tracking services.

## 5. Data Security

We prioritize simplicity and privacy by design. Since no user data is stored, the risk of data exposure is minimized.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

GOAT QR Generator does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

## 8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the project repository or deployment page.
